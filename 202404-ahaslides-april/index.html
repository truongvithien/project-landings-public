What is AhaSlides?

AhaSlides is an interactive presentation platform that helps you engage your audience with polls, quizzes, Q&A, word clouds, and more.

What is the Plus Plan?

The Plus Plan is a paid subscription tier offering additional features like advanced analytics, branding options, priority support, and increased audience participation limits.

Is the 3-month free trial for the Plus Plan truly free?

Yes! There are no credit card details required to sign up for the free trial. You can try all the Plus Plan features for 3 months without any obligation.

What happens after the free trial ends?

After the trial, you will automatically revert to the Free Plan unless you choose to upgrade to a paid Plus Plan subscription.

Are there any limitations on the Free Plan?

The free plan allows you to create and present as many times as you like, but it has a limit of 7 active participants at a time.
